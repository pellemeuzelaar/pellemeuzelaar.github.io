[Hi!]

These are some of my projects. The first two are related to data-analysis and optimisation and were written in Python. The assignments were carried out during the Technology and Operations Management (TOM) master's program. The others are projects that mainly demonstrate my front-end development capabilities.

[CCUS supply chain for master's thesis]

For my master's thesis, I designed a supply chain for CCUS (Carbon Capture, Utilization & Storage) across six European countries. The supply chain activities include capturing CO₂ emissions from industry and utilizing it for the production of urea, e-methanol and Sustainable Aviation Fuel whilst storing the remainder underground. Using a mathematical model in Python, I optimized the supply chain to minimize costs. Scenario analysis based on qualitative research introduced practical constraints. The project demonstrated how cross-border collaboration can create a more cost-effective supply chain which significantly reduces industrial CO₂ emissions. The project was awarded a 7 out of 10.

[Belsimpel warehouse allocation problem]

During the course Data Analysis and Programming, I was tasked with allocating tablets and smartphones to a new warehouse for Belsimpel. This expansion allowed Belsimpel to increase product sales over the long term, although it inevitably led to longer shipping times. Using Python querying, I minimized profit losses resulting from these increased delivery times. The assignment was graded a 7.2 out of 10.

[Band member search form]

This project provides a way for musicians to connect and form a band. The user can submit their preferences and the answers will be listed publically. This allows others looking for a specific player's attributes to connect with them and form a band or musical group. Eventually this project may be developed into a platform for multiple users. It currently only supports the data submission of a single user. This project was made out of a personal interest as I am a guitarist myself and I would like to connect with other musicians.

[Fender Strat Documentation Page]
